Powered by Arduino Uno

The researcher's propose a device to promote safety among those people who clean sewers and drainages.

    Commonly here in the philippines, local government units clean the sewers and drainage by hiring men clean them manually, exposing them to great danger of diseases that may acquire from the contaminants that is present on the waste water flowing through the sewer.

    The objective of the project is to design an effective device of cleaning sewers without direct human contact with harmful substances present in the water. The system will be using Arduino-based technology to design an electronic device that can automate the process of cleaning the solid waste that have been accumulated overtime.

    The electronic device will be controlled via a remote-control system using an arduino application with camera footage for ease of usage and will have two sets of wheels for mobility the solid wastes will be collected by a lifter at the front of the device with small holes to separate solid wastes from harmful drainage water. The wastes will be stored at a waste bin located at the top of the device with an indicator if the bin is full. The devive will be enclosed by a casing to prevent the electrical parts to touch the water and will run on built in battery.
